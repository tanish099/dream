FAQs

Do you shoot as well as edit? ＋
Yes — full‑service. We offer on‑site shoots (all cameras/gimbals) and professional editing. You can also send your raw clips for editing only.
How fast is delivery? ＋
Standard 48–72 hours after receiving footage. Express delivery available (same/next day) at an additional fee.
Can you work with trending sounds? ＋
Absolutely. We match cuts to trending audio, beats, or VO. We can also source royalty‑free alternatives if required.
What do you need from me? ＋
Your footage (Drive/WeTransfer) or a shoot booking, plus a short brief: mood/tempo, references, brand colors, text details, and deadlines.
